What is Wave Computing?

Wave Computing is a paradigm in which complex problems are solved through the physics of waves, not only through sequential switching. Superposition and interference let many pathways coexist and interact at once in the electromagnetic field—aiming at a different cost–performance–scaling envelope than conventional architectures for the hardest problems in science, engineering, and AI.
